GENTLE MONSTER X SON7



Nice One Sonny, Nice One Son


Gentle Monster presents a special project with football player Son Heung-min.

Son Heung-min, setting new standards in global sports, and Gentle Monster, relentlessly experimenting in the arts, meet for a collaboration that extends the limits of two independent fields.


In commemoration of this collaboration, Gentle Monster creates a pop-up at Haus Dosan featuring a sculpture of Son Heung-min’s signature ceremony pose and other original elements.



Haus Dosan, 50, Apgujeong-ro 46-gil, Gangnam-gu, Seoul

July 5 - August 10, 2022

Mon - Sun 11:00 AM – 9:00 PM

SON 7 Special Package

A special package, only available at Haus Dosan, is designed for exhibition only and consists of an incense chamber that embodies Son Heung-min’s signature ceremony pose and exclusive eyewear. The SON 7 collection, consisting of two sunglasses, is made from acetate and has tinted lenses in Gentle Monster’s unique designs with “GENTLE MONSTER X SON 7” engraved in the temple.


The SON 7 pop up featuring art sculptures and exclusive packages will be open at Haus Dosan from July 5 to August 8.
